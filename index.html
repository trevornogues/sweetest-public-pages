What is Sweetest?

A small social app for the people in your life who actually have opinions about croissants, cookies, ice cream, and matcha. Build your personal top-10 across every sweet category, follow your friends, and watch the city's collective ranking emerge from everyone's picks.

  • Personal rankings. Every category — bakeries, ice cream, boba, donuts — is its own top-10. Add a treat, compare it head-to-head against a few you've already ranked, and your list reorders automatically.
  • Friends-only feed. See what the people you actually trust just ranked, with notes and photos. No influencers, no algorithm.
  • City & region leaderboards. Your rankings quietly contribute to anonymous city-wide bests. Find out whether the place you love is also the place everyone else loves.
  • Private by default if you want it. Flip privacy on and only your followers see your lists — your votes still count toward city leaderboards anonymously.

How to join

  1. Tap the button above on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. If prompted, install Apple's TestFlight app (it's free) — this is how Apple distributes pre-release apps.
  3. Open the link again, tap Accept, then Install. Sweetest will appear on your home screen.
