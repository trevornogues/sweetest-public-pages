Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Trevor Nogues ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Sweetest mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address, password (stored only in hashed form by Firebase Authentication), and a username you choose.
  • Profile settings. Your privacy setting (public or private profile) and any custom categories you create.
  • User-generated content. The places, rankings (Top 5 entries), cities, optional notes, and optional photos you add to the App.
  • Social graph. The accounts you choose to follow.
  • Communications. If you contact us by email, we receive the contents of that email.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data. Our backend provider (Google Firebase) may collect basic technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and crash logs to keep the service running and secure.
  • Authentication metadata. Sign-in timestamps and authentication tokens needed to keep you signed in.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs and we do not collect precise device location.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Create and authenticate your account.
  • Display your rankings, photos, and profile to you and to other users in accordance with your privacy setting.
  • Aggregate rankings by city to show city-level lists.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • With other users. If your profile is set to public, your username, profile, rankings, photos, and follow relationships are visible to other Sweetest users. If your profile is private, only users you approve can see your content. (Where the App offers a private setting; check the Profile screen for current options.)
  • City rankings. Aggregated, non-personally-identifiable counts of rankings per city may be shown to all users.
  • Service providers. We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage) to host the App's data and run the App. Firebase processes data on our behalf under Google's terms; see Firebase's Privacy and Security page for details.
  • Legal and safety. We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that doing so is necessary to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfers. If Sweetest is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, primarily in the United States. We use Firebase's security rules and authentication to restrict access so that, by default, you can only read and write your own data. No method of storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or de-identify your account information and user-generated content within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. Aggregate city-level statistics that do not identify you may be retained.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction. You can view and update much of your information directly in the App (profile, rankings, photos, custom categories).
  • Deletion. You can request deletion of your account and associated content by emailing noguestj@gmail.com. We will verify the request and complete deletion within a reasonable period.
  • California, EU/UK and other jurisdictions. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights such as the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the processing of your personal information, or to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, email noguestj@gmail.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Sweetest is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at noguestj@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective date" above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Your continued use of the App after the changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
